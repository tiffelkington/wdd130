About Rapid Decisions

Who are we?

Just beyond the neon pulse of Las Vegas, Rapid Decisions offers a different kind of thrill: a chance to trade crowded sidewalks for open water on the lower Colorado River. Whether you want a lively guided float with splashy stretches and a little adrenaline, or a calm drift through cool desert air with canyon views and wildlife all around, every trip is designed to feel like the perfect reset from the city. Our guides know how to match the mood, from adventurous and laugh loud to slow, scenic, and quietly unforgettable, so whether you came to Nevada for excitement or escape, the river delivers both.

History

Founded in the early 1990s, Desert Current Rafting started when two local river guides began taking friends on informal float trips along the lower Colorado River, sharing canyon stories and hidden wildlife spots along the way. What began with one used raft and word-of-mouth recommendations quickly grew into a small guiding company known for relaxed, scenic trips.

Over the years, the company became known for friendly guides, sunset tours, and a tradition of naming each raft after desert weather. Returning guests especially came to love the guides’ habit of pointing out quiet coves and telling stories about the river’s changing moods from season to season. Even now, they like to say every trip should feel part adventure, part local storytelling.
