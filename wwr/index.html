Plan Your Trip Today!

Experience the thrill, the beauty, and the unforgettable rush of the Colorado River with Rapid Decisions. Whether you're a first-time rafter looking for a fun, scenic float or an adrenaline-seeker ready to tackle exciting rapids, we have the perfect trip waiting for you.

At Rapid Decisions, your safety and enjoyment come first. Our expert guides are highly trained, passionate about the river, and dedicated to creating an experience that’s as safe as it is exhilarating. We match every guest with the right trip for their skill level and adventure style, so you can relax, have fun, and make the most of every moment on the water.

Don’t settle for an ordinary day—choose an experience you’ll be talking about long after the river settles. Your adventure starts here.

Let's Get Started!

Our guides love what they do and have collectively over 100 seasons of rafting experience. They know the river and the banks. They know how to best keep rafters out of trouble. And best of all they have a passion for helping folks have a really fun time on the river!

Not only do we guide you to and through a tour that matches your skills and abilities, we also ensure you have safety instructions, gear, and rafting guides certified in first aid and CPR. We inspect our rafts and oars between each run. You can count on Rapid Decisions to get you safely through each trip.
