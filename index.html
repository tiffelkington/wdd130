Nevada

The State of Nevada in the United States is filled with lofty mountain ranges and deep basins, open blue skies, and desert plants and animals. Nevada is home to cattle ranches, alfalfa farms, mining, hunting, and casinos. We have beautiful, snow-capped mountains in the western side of the states for skiing and visiting Lake Tahoe. Down in the southern tip we have the Las Vegas Strip, home of the flash and sparkle of themed casinos depicting the Great Pyramid of Egypt; the hustle of New York, New York; Paris; the Sphere; Bellagio fountains; Caesar's Palace; and the MGM Emerald City. Much of the state is covered in sage brush, pinion pines, and joshua trees. And, yes, there are a few places that are great for rafting. There's the Truckee River in the western side of the state and the Colorado River in the south.
